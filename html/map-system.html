Maxell Airtight Products Sealing Line

M.A.P. System

Valley Construction Supply is a major wholesaler and supplier for Maxell Airtight Products (M.A.P.) in the Seattle and Pacific Northwest market.

We carry their air sealer, pipe and wall sealer, and flashing tape products which are all-rubber, single self adhesive tapes that make sheathing joints airtight and waterproof in fewer steps.

The MAP System is easier to use and produces less waste than other sealing products on the market which need to be nailed or taped on to produce the seal.

Maxell Airtight Products (M.A.P.) has been a major contributer in the construction industry by creating products with incredible efficiency, workability, and environmental impacts from their design to their produciton and sale.

Benefits of using Maxell Airtight Products

  • No nailing or needing to be taped on to use.
  • Grommet is ALL rubber and has a self-adhesive peel. and stick backing that requires only one step to install.
  • Saves on time and labor.
  • Significantly less waste.

Pipe & Wall Sealing Products

Valley Construction Supply also carries the Maxell line of pipe and wall sealer products that creates an air-tight and waterproof adhesive sheet to be applied to pipes that pass through building's walls and to the outside.

  1. There is pipe penetration through the wall.
  2. Put Pipe & Wall Sealer on the pipe.
  3. Peel off the release paper
  4. Apply the pressure by hand or roller.
