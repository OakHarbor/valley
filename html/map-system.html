M.A.P. Air Sealing and Flashing Tape Products

M.A.P. System

Valley Construction Supply is a major wholesaler and supplier for Maxwell Airtight Products (M.A.P.) in the Seattle and Pacific Northwest market. Maxwell is most famous for their cassette tapes but have been manufacturing and selling air-tight and flashing products for decades.

We carry their air sealing and flashing tape products which are single adhesive tapes that make sheathing joints airtight and waterproof that is easier to use and produces less waste than other sealing products on the market.

Maxwell Airtight Products (M.A.P.) has been a major contributer in the construction industry by creating products with incredible efficiency, workability, and environmental impacts from their design to their produciton and sale.
