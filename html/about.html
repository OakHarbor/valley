Valley's History

In 1982 Cliff Beatty founded Valley Construction Supply with the sole purpose of providing the region’s contractors with a reliable source for building materials at competitive prices. Being built on the ideals of honesty, dedication and leadership, almost 40 years later Valley Construction Supply is still family owned and operated by Cliff’s son, Mark Beatty who continues with the same values. With a fleet of 25 trucks and two conveniently located warehouses, Pacific and Marysville, we are able to provide our customers with next day delivery. Our dedicated sales staff has an average of 25 years’ experience which gives them the ability to react to our customer’s needs. Valley Construction Supply is committed to putting customer service as a top priority in an ever changing building environment.

Need a contractor? We know contractors

If your home builder needs a quality foundation, excavator or a framing contractor we have those strong relationships to pass on, we do the same for our long time customers looking for new business and new relationships. We call it the art of “NETWORKING” Connecting hard working, integrity driven pro contractors with the top quality home builders in the greater Puget sound area. At the end of the day “ all we have is our word “ and we at Valley Construction Supply never take that lightly.

Our Mission:

We strive to be loyal to our partners, build long lasting relationships with our customers, built on integrity while offering top-notch next day service to our customers. We have always offered fair pricing and spot on billing and most importantly our word is our bond.
